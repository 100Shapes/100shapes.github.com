Tweevie

Enjoy the best of twitter whilst watching TV. We’ve long enjoyed the hilarity that reading twitter comments can induce whilst watching BBC Newsnight or the poignancy it can bring to an X Factor final. Tweevie is what happens when your television and twitter get steamy under the sheets. Here’s how we did it...

Smart Gestures

Tweetbot has gestures that can save you time. 1) Tap a Link or Avatar in your timeline to view more. 2) Tapping and holding any of those elements gives you options. 3) And finally you can configure triple-tap to reply-to, fave, retweet, or even translate a tweet.
